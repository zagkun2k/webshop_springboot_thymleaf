Is payment at YeeShop safe? Does YeeShop store customer card information?

You can be completely assured because when paying online at YeeShop, we implement a guaranteed payment policy. Important features of secure payment:
   • Secure payment with VeriSign security system.
   • Track the delivery of goods.
   • Money is only transferred to the seller after you confirm receipt of the goods.
Your credit card information will be kept securely by Sendo because we use secure Digital Certificates (SSL) technology to encrypt all personal data sent over the Internet from your electronic device. All personal information is protected by a firewall.

How can I cancel my order?

Step 1: Login to your YeeShop account.
Step 2: In the top right corner of the screen, please tap your name. Then continue to click "Order information".
Step 3: Select "Waiting for confirmation".
Step 4: At the order you want to cancel, click the "Cancel" button, choose a reason and continue to press the cancel button. Note: Orders can only be canceled when the order has been placed within 8 hours!
